> Replace all `[PLACEHOLDER]` values with your client's details before using.

## Prompt 1: Pillar Blog Outline + Draft Generator

**Use with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini

```
Act as a senior SEO content strategist and writer.

Generate a complete pillar blog outline AND a full draft for:

Business: [BUSINESS NAME]
Location: [CITY, STATE]
Primary Service: [SERVICE]
Primary Keyword: [KEYWORD]
Target Word Count: [WORD COUNT]
Target Audience: [AUDIENCE]

PART 1 — OUTLINE:
- Include 1 H1, 6–8 H2s, and 3–5 H3s
- Embed the primary keyword naturally in the H1 and at least 2 H2 headings
- Include sections for: introduction, what it is, benefits, cost breakdown, 
  procedure/process, why choose us (local trust signals), FAQ, and CTA
- Add 3 internal link suggestions (to supporting blogs or service pages)
- Write a meta description (max 155 characters) including the primary keyword

PART 2 — FULL DRAFT:
- Write the complete blog post following the outline
- Use a helpful, friendly, expert tone (not salesy)
- Include the primary keyword 4–6 times naturally
- Add one comparison table and one numbered list
- End with a strong, specific call-to-action for the business
- Total length: [WORD COUNT] words
```

## Prompt 2: Supporting Blog Generator

**Use with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini

```
Act as an SEO content writer who specialises in local business blogs.

Write a [WORD COUNT]-word blog post for:

Business: [BUSINESS NAME] in [CITY]
Blog Title: [TITLE]
Primary Keyword: [KEYWORD]
Search Intent: [INFORMATIONAL / COMMERCIAL / TRANSACTIONAL]
Blog Type: [FAQ / Location / Service / Problem-Solution / Comparison]
Target Audience: [AUDIENCE]

Requirements:
- Open with a compelling introduction that addresses the reader's problem or question
- Use the primary keyword in the H1, first 100 words, and 2–3 H2 headings
- Include H2 and H3 subheadings for scannability
- Write in short paragraphs (2–4 sentences each)
- Add one practical tip box or callout section
- End with a natural, specific CTA for the business
- Add one internal link to the pillar blog: "[PILLAR BLOG TITLE]"
- Do NOT use generic filler phrases like "in today's digital world" or "in conclusion"
```

## Prompt 3: Local SEO Keyword Pack Generator

**Use with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini

```
Act as a local SEO specialist with 10+ years of experience in India.

Generate a complete keyword pack for:

Business Type: [BUSINESS TYPE]
City: [CITY]
State: [STATE]
Primary Service: [SERVICE]

Provide the following, formatted as tables:

TABLE 1 — PRIMARY KEYWORDS (3 keywords)
Columns: Keyword | Monthly Search Volume (estimate) | Search Intent | Difficulty

TABLE 2 — SECONDARY KEYWORDS (5 keywords)
Columns: Keyword | Search Intent | Best Content Type

TABLE 3 — LONG-TAIL KEYWORDS (4 keywords, question format)
Columns: Keyword | Intent | Why It Converts

TABLE 4 — LOCAL SEARCH TERMS (5 keywords)
Columns: Keyword | Target Page | Google Feature It May Trigger

TABLE 5 — PEOPLE ALSO ASK (6 questions Google shows for this topic)
Columns: Question | Answer Format (paragraph / list / table)

Also provide:
- 3 target neighbourhoods/areas within [CITY] for hyperlocal content
- 3 Google Business Profile optimisation tips for this business type
```

## Prompt 4: Content Cluster Planner

**Use with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini

```
Act as an SEO content strategist at a top digital marketing agency.

Create a full content cluster plan for:

Business: [BUSINESS NAME]
Location: [CITY]
Industry/Niche: [NICHE]
Main Topic: [PILLAR TOPIC]
Business Goal: [LEADS / TRAFFIC / TRUST / ALL]

Generate the following:

1. PILLAR BLOG
   - Title (include primary keyword + city)
   - Primary keyword
   - Search intent
   - Target word count
   - Meta description (max 155 chars)

2. CONTENT CLUSTER TABLE (5 supporting blogs)
   Columns: Blog Title | Target Keyword | Search Intent | Blog Type | Word Count | Links To

3. INTERNAL LINKING MAP
   Show how the 6 pieces link to each other (pillar ↔ supporting blogs)

4. CONTENT CALENDAR
   Suggest a 6-week publishing schedule with priority order

5. QUICK WINS
   3 blog topics that could rank within 30 days due to low competition

Make the plan ready to hand directly to a client or content team.
```
